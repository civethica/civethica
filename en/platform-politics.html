Manifesto of Planetary Governance

Politics is not a struggle for power. It is the collective responsibility for the health of our common home. We reject the outdated model of geopolitics, where fortress-nations compete for resources. We propose a new paradigm: geo-governance—a collaborative, science-based, and ethically-aligned process of caring for the 'Humanity + Biosphere' system.
